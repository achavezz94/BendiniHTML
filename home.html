About Bendini

Since September of 2002, Bendini Fine Men's Clothing has strived to offer the Bergen County and Tri-State area market place the finest quality and personal service that is seldom experienced these days. Bendini focused on delivering high quality service as well as fine men's clothing. It has been noticed by Bergen Health & Life as the "Best Independent specialty store for men in Bergen" in 2012.
